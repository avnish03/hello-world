• The icon of app will appear to customers in the store and should be same as app icon you enter later in iTunes Connect.
  • A customer should instantly associate the app icon that installs on their devices.
  • The app icon is also the image that will appear in Springboard when the app is installed.
  • The app icon should be image of size 120x120 in jpg, jpeg or png format only.
