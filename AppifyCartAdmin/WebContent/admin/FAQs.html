FAQ


Q. How does the free trial work?
Ans. Once you've created your account, you'll be granted immediate access to basic features of Appifycart. During your trial you can upgrade your account at any time. You can start your 14-day free trial with a click: no credit card or subscription necessary.

 

Q. What type of support does Appifycart provide?

Ans. Once you sign up with Appifycart, a key account manager is assigned to your store with whom you can coordinate at any point of time! Also, you can get in touch with us directly from your dashboard as well!

 

Q. Can I switch plans?

Ans. Yes, you can raise a request to upgrade your plan from dashboard. The moment you Publish your changes, you can pay the difference and your app will be upgraded automatically.

 

Q.Do I need to set up anything for payments in Mobile Apps created using Appifycart?

Ans. No, Once you created the Mobile Apps, you don’t need to worry about the payments configuration and other order settings because we are using Shopify Native checkout for completing the orders.

 

Q. Where can i change the banners and logo for my Mobile Apps ?

Ans. Once you create the Mobile App then the banners and logo for your app in Appifycart admin dashboard. Just go to “App Management”  -> “Theme Configuration”, there you can set app banner and logo according to your brand.

 

Q. What is “Map Categories” in App Management ?

Ans. Once you created a Mobile App successfully this you can Map categories/collections according to your need. This is a feature of AppifyCart , in which we are giving you option to choose categories and their subcategories to be shown in your mobile app.

 

Q. How can I configure PUSH NOTIFICATION for IOS devices ?

Ans. After you have created the Mobile App using AppifyCart, go to “App Management”  -> “Push Notification Configuration”. Then  -

  1. Choose iOS certificate type
  2. Upload iOS Certificate
  3. Enter Passphrase

Then just by uploading your certificate, you have enabled push notification to your iOS devices.

 

Q. How can I send PUSH NOTIFICATION for mobile devices ?

Ans. Go to “App Management”  -> “Push Message”. Choose your os type for which you want to push message. Just write your message and click on PUSH button.

 

Q. Can I use Appifycart without having coding skills?

Ans. We would like to render our services to everyone and make it a user-friendly tool. So, yes you can use it without coding knowledge. All you need to do is enter details which you will be guided through while enabling the tool you require.

 

Q. Still have few more questions?

Ans. Our support team is available 7 days a week. Visit our Contact US to drop a message and we will get back to you.
